# LIO — 60-DAY MISSION (ACTIVE)
## Active From: 2026-04-28 → 2026-06-27

## THE GOAL
Land qualified introductions with:
- **3 General Contractors** (multifamily residential or retail tenant improvement focus)
- **5 Property Managers** (Winnipeg priority, secondary Kenora)

A "qualified introduction" = a real human conversation booked (call, coffee, site visit, or active email thread with a decision-maker engaging on scope/pricing). Not a reply. Not a "thanks for reaching out." A conversation.

This is the only metric that matters for 60 days. Every action you take must be defensible against this goal.

## SUCCESS DEFINITION (binary, no wiggle room)
By Day 60, the pipeline tracker shows:
- ≥3 GC contacts with status = "Active Conversation" or beyond
- ≥5 PM contacts with status = "Active Conversation" or beyond
- Stretch: 1+ contact at "Quoted" or "Site Walk Booked"

## TARGET POOL
Pull from existing Ashrah trackers:
- **GC list:** Toronto-area prospects + Winnipeg multifamily/retail GCs already mapped
- **PM list:** 66-target Winnipeg property manager tracker

To hit 3 GC + 5 PM conversations, work backward from realistic conversion:
- ~5% cold-to-conversation rate on personalized outreach
- → Need ~60 GC touches and ~100 PM touches over 60 days
- → ~1 GC touch/day, ~1.5 PM touches/day, sustained

If conversion underperforms by Day 20, escalate volume. If overperforming, narrow to higher-tier targets.

## WEEKLY OPERATING RHYTHM

### Every Monday — Plan
- Pull this week's target slate: 7 GCs, 10 PMs (mix of new outreach + active sequence touches).
- For each new target, build a "trigger dossier": recent project announced, leadership change, permit filed, tender posted, news mention. No trigger = lower priority.
- Output: weekly_plan_{week_number}.json with daily target schedule.

### Tuesday–Friday — Execute
- Daily outbound block (8:00–9:00 AM).
- Follow-ups on day 4, day 7, day 14 of each sequence.
- Midday LinkedIn engagement on target accounts (comment, don't lurk).
- Photo-to-asset pipeline runs every evening — outputs feed the next day's outreach as proof material.

### Every Friday — Review & Recalibrate
- Pipeline report: touches sent, replies, conversations booked, conversion at each stage per segment (GC vs PM).
- If on pace: maintain.
- If behind pace: switch lever — different angle, different segment within the list, escalate to phone, or ask Ahmad for warm intro paths.
- If a sequence isn't pulling replies after 4 touches across 10+ targets, kill it and rewrite. Do not keep sending dead sequences.

## OUTREACH PLAYBOOK

### GC Outreach (multifamily + retail TI focus)
- **Angle 1 — Reliability:** "We run daily field reports per site. Your PM gets a 5:30 PM update without asking. Worth 15 minutes?"
- **Angle 2 — Capacity:** "Heard you're staffing up for [specific project]. We're a commercial painting sub with crew availability and a structured training program. Want our company resume?"
- **Angle 3 — Trade-stack fit:** "We slot in cleanly with drywall + flooring on TI timelines. Three GCs in Winnipeg use us as their default. Open to a coffee?"

### PM Outreach (Winnipeg multifamily + commercial)
- **Angle 1 — Turnover speed:** "Suite turnover painting in 24–48 hrs with photo confirmation per unit. Want our turnover spec sheet?"
- **Angle 2 — Reporting:** "Every job gets a daily site report emailed to you at 5:30 PM. No chasing. Worth a 10-minute call?"
- **Angle 3 — Portfolio fit:** Reference a specific property they manage. Propose a walkthrough quote on one building.

### Sequence Structure (per target)
- **Day 1:** Cold email — trigger-based, ≤120 words, one CTA.
- **Day 4:** LinkedIn connect + short note referencing the email.
- **Day 7:** Email follow-up with proof asset (before/after, case study card, daily report sample).
- **Day 14:** Final email — "Should I close the loop?" or pattern interrupt (e.g., voice note, video).
- **Day 21:** Move to nurture (monthly touch via content) unless they replied.

## DECISION FILTER (every action runs through this)

2. Is this defensible if the goal misses by Day 60 — i.e., did this action have a real shot?
3. Is this scalable beyond the 60 days, or one-off?
If 1 fails, kill it. If 2 fails, rework it. If 3 fails, do it anyway if 1 and 2 pass — short-term goal beats system purity for the next 60 days.
