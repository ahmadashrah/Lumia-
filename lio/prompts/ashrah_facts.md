## Brand & domains

- **ashrahpainting.ca** — the painting subcontractor brand. This is the company that bids the work, runs the crews, and signs the contracts. All outreach to GCs and PMs comes from this brand.
- **ashrah.ai** — the technology brand. This is where the AI estimator and the Lumia operations layer live (auto site reports, ask-Lumia chat, 6-day delay forecasting).

These map directly to the tagline:
- *"We run like a tech company"* → **ashrah.ai** (Lumi + Lumia)
- *"We paint like tradespeople"* → **ashrahpainting.ca** (trained crews, clean finishes, no surface damage)

The two-domain structure is itself a credibility signal: most painting subs don't have a tech sister-brand.

## Operational system (use these as proof, not as the headline)

Built on data from **65 completed projects** over the past 3 years across multi-family, office, institutional, and retail builds. Ashrah is a Manitoba-based **finishing company** — not a typical painting company. We finish projects.

- **Lumi (AI estimating agent)** — You send drawings; Lumi returns a **95% accurate estimate for both painting AND professional cleaning in 24 hours**. Because there's no human estimator on payroll, our bids land **at least 13% lower** than human-estimated competing painting subs. Pitch to estimators: "we make YOUR bid book faster and your number lower."
- **Lumia (AI operations agent)** — Once a job is awarded, Lumia builds the schedule from real-time project data, picks the crew best suited for the job, and notifies them and the PM by name with on-site arrival time.
- **Daily plan + safety gate** — Every morning the crew receives their daily plan. They cannot start work until they submit a photo wearing proper safety gear for the task.
- **6 PM daily report** — At 6 PM Winnipeg time the PM gets a detailed report on what was completed that day plus tomorrow's plan. Auto-sent. No chasing.
- **Ask-Lumia chat** — PM (or GC) can ask Lumia anything about production, timelines, or quality, 24/7, and get a current answer.
- **6-day delay forecast** — Lumia flags potential delays six days in advance and gives a path to stay on schedule (reallocate resources, pull the PM in early).
- **3-day on-site emergency forecast** — Lumia flags on-site emergencies three days out so they can be solved before they hit the calendar.
- **Professional cleaning crew** — Alerted a week ahead, trained to deliver a spotless site that represents the client's name well.
- **Painter-on-site through cleaning** — A painter stays on site through the entire cleaning phase. If a cleaner dents a wall or scuffs a finished surface, it gets handled on the spot. No budget creep, no delays from punch-list.
- **Structured painter training** — Crews run on a documented training program, so finish quality is consistent across sites and across crews.
