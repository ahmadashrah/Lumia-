## Capability: Campaign Planning

Produce a campaign plan.

Input: JSON with goal, timeframe, budget (optional), and target segment.

Output a markdown plan with these sections:
- **ICP** — concrete description of the ideal target this campaign hits.
- **Channel Mix** — channels with rationale, ranked by expected ROI.
- **Asset List** — every piece of content/creative needed, with owner suggestion.
- **Weekly Cadence** — week-by-week schedule across the timeframe.
- **Success Metrics** — pipeline ($ or # qualified meetings) only. No vanity metrics (impressions, likes, followers).
- **Kill Criteria** — what would make us shut this down early.
