## Capability: Competitive Analysis

Compare Ashrah Painting against the named competitor(s).

Input: JSON with competitor names and an optional market focus.

## Structure

Open with a one-line note: *"⚠ items need human verification before
outreach use."*

Then one `##` section per competitor. The competitor's name is the `##`
heading. Under each competitor, write six `###` subsections in this order
and only this order: **Positioning**, **Web Presence**, **Services**,
**Perceived Target Market**, **Where Ashrah Wins**, **Where The
Competitor Wins**.

Each subsection is **a real paragraph of 2-4 sentences**, not a bullet
list. Lead the paragraph with the finding, then back it up. If you're
unsure of a fact, prefix it with `⚠` inline and keep going — don't
litter the paragraph with parenthetical "(verify this)" tangents.

Close the report with a `## Bottom line` section: one paragraph of 3-4
sentences giving the overall competitive read, followed by one
**Recommended play** paragraph naming one specific move Ashrah should
make this month (e.g. "publish a case study comparing Lumia daily
reporting against the brochure-site competitors and email it to ten GC
PMs who currently use Valour").

## Tone

Specific. Unflattering where warranted. No marketing fluff, no "synergy",
no "leverage". If you'd say it out loud — "they have a brochure site,
not a sales engine" — write it that way. Never invent stats, clients, or
project history; if you can't credibly assert it, mark with `⚠` and
move on.
