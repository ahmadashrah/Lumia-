# MASTER EMAIL TEMPLATE — Long-form, Ahmad-voiced

This is the canonical client-outreach email for Ashrah Painting. Use it whenever
the recipient is a client, GC, PM, developer, or estimator and the context calls
for a substantive intro (not a quick cold-open).

**Sender identity:** This email is signed as Ahmad Ashrah personally. The From
line should read "Ahmad Ashrah" with the appropriate sending mailbox.

**Personalization rule:** Replace `[Client name]` with the recipient's first name.
Optionally replace `[Company]` placeholders if you have firm-specific anchors
worth weaving in. Otherwise leave the body verbatim — this email is written to
land on its own without per-company custom paragraphs.

**Length:** ~450 words. Long for cold outreach but written as a story with a
specific ask (15 minutes) at the end. Trades open-rate for reply-quality.

---

## Body

```
Hi [Client name],

I hope this email finds you well. My name is Lio Ashrah, Business Development
Representative at Ashrah Painting, a Manitoba-based finishing company.

We're not your typical painting company. We finish projects, and let me
explain what that means.

Over the past 3 years, we've collected data from 65 projects across
multi-family, office, institutional, and retail builds. In almost every one,
things got messy at the end. PMs stressed out coordinating between painters,
cleaners, and electricians. Jobs running long. Deliveries missed, and the
GC's reputation taking the hit. We also saw overworked PMs chasing trades
with no answer, stress levels climbing, production slipping, and burnout
setting in.

We understand how much a clean, on-time, on-budget finish matters. It's
what wins your client for life. That's why we built Lumia.

Lumia is our AI platform, built from scratch and backed by data from those
65 projects. You send us the drawings, and we get to work.

Within 24 hours, you get a 95% accurate estimate for both painting and
professional cleaning. Once approved, Lumia builds a detailed schedule from
real-time project data. It picks the crew best suited for the job and
notifies both them and the PM by name, with their on-site arrival time.

Every morning, our team gets their daily plan, so timelines are addressed
and respected. They can't start work until they submit a photo wearing the
proper safety gear for the task. At 6 PM, the PM gets a detailed report on
what was completed that day, along with tomorrow's plan.

Delays happen, and we know what they cost a GC's reputation. Lumia flags
potential delays 6 days in advance and gives us a path to stay on schedule,
whether that's reallocating resources or pulling the PM in early. It also
flags on-site emergencies 3 days out so we can solve them before they hit
the calendar.

Because PMs run tight schedules, we built a feature where they can ask Lumia
any question about production, timelines, or quality, 24/7.

When painting wraps, our professional cleaning crew is alerted a week ahead.
They're trained to deliver a spotless site that represents your name well.
We also keep a painter on site through the entire cleaning phase, so if a
cleaner dents a wall or scuffs a surface, we handle it on the spot. No
budget creep, no delays.

If you want to protect your reputation and your budgets, I'd be glad to grab
15 minutes to walk you through it.
```

## Subject line options (≤8 words each)

Pick one based on recipient:
- "Protect your reputation and your budgets" (default — matches the close)
- "How we finish projects without budget creep" (curiosity-led)
- "An AI platform built for your PMs" (Lumia-led)
- "Finishing company for [Company name]" (warm/known firms)

## Sign-off

**Do not include any sign-off or signature in the draft body.** The Zoho mailbox
is configured to append the signature server-side at send time. Drafts end at
the CTA line ("...I'd be glad to grab 15 minutes to walk you through it.").
