DAY 2 — 5

Schedule & Forecast

We phase the work so your building stays operational — after-hours, weekends, zoned shifts. The schedule is loaded into your portal with a rolling 6-day delay forecast for weather, supply and crew. You see risk before it hits.

  • Phased plan, shareable PDF
  • 6-day forward forecast
  • Tenant / ops notice templates included

DAY 5 — 7

Color & Coating Selection

Sign-off on color codes, sheens, and specialty coatings (epoxy, urethane, anti-corrosive, anti-microbial). Every code is logged with the room it touches — and stays in the portal for the life of the building.

  • SW / BM trade-line supplied
  • Sheens locked per surface
  • Specialty coatings spec'd by environment

Masking, drop cloths, baseboard pull, lift / scaffold set, machinery isolation. Around production lines, we work with your ops team to mask without shutting you down.

  • Daily safety check-in on portal

05
SURFACE PREP

Patch, Sand, Prime

Drywall patches, nail-pops, hairline cracks repaired. Spot-prime or full prime depending on substrate. Surface temp and humidity logged before primer goes on.

  • Surface temp / RH on every coat day
  • Bonding primer for problem substrates
  • Photos uploaded by end of shift
06
COATS

First & Second Coat

Two-coat standard. Cut-in by hand, roll or spray depending on surface and specialty coating. Crew check-in via GPS at start and end of shift.

  • 2-coat standard, 3 on dark-over-light
  • Crew GPS check-in
  • Cure / recoat timing logged
07
QC

Quality Control Walk

Foreman walks every elevation at end of each coat. Touch-ups marked with painter's tape, photographed, addressed before sign-off. Property manager invited to walk at end of Phase 2.

  • Per-elevation QC photo set
  • Punch-list opens in portal
  • Client walk on milestones
08
DETAIL

Trim, Doors & Detail

Trim, doors, frames, baseboards, ceiling lines. Different sheen, different paint, separate record in the portal. This is where most jobs lose hours — we plan it as its own phase.

CLEANING

Post-construction Cleaning

For new builds and renovations we take the room from paint-done to hand-off-ready: dust pass on all horizontal surfaces, fixtures wiped, glass scraped, paint flecks pulled off floors, finish detailing.

You walk the building with the foreman. Anything you flag is photographed, added to the punch-list, and closed before invoice. No retention games — when the list is closed, the job is done.

  • Walk + punch-list on portal
  • Touch-up paint left for owner
  • Invoice tied to closed punch-list

06 / Standards
Standards

What
"done"
means.

Our default specs. Anything different is called out explicitly in your quote — no surprises buried in the line items.

Coat count
2 coats standard. 3 on dark-over-light, on bare drywall, or on transition between substrates.
Paint lines
Sherwin-Williams Pro & Benjamin Moore Trade. Other brands on request.
Sheens
Flat (ceiling) · Eggshell (walls) · Satin (high-touch) · Semi-gloss (trim, doors) · Gloss (mechanical / industrial).
Specialty coatings
Epoxy floor & wall, polyurethane, anti-corrosive, anti-microbial, intumescent, elastomeric.
Surface conditions
Coats applied between 10 – 30 °C, RH 30 – 70 %. Outside that range, work is rescheduled and logged.
Prep
All nail-pops, hairline cracks, dings patched, sanded, primed. Caulk renewed at trim & ceiling joints.
Around machinery
Equipment shrouded, isolated and signed off by your ops team before any spray work begins.
Touch-up paint
1 L of each color (or matching can size) labeled with room + code, left with owner at hand-off.
Documentation
Daily log, photo set, paint records, punch-list — pushed to portal in real time.
